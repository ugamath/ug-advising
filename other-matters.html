Common questions
I’m not sure where my question fits. What should I do?

Use this tab and describe the issue; we will route it to the correct category internally if needed.

Does this include advising on course planning in general?

Yes—general advising that does not specifically fall under the other categories can be submitted here.

Can I submit multiple issues in one query?

Prefer one issue per submission. If they are tightly related, you may include them together, but clarity helps.
