FAQ

Common questions
How do I choose a thesis supervisor/topic?

Submit your interests, the courses you have completed, and any initial proposal. If you already contacted a faculty member, mention that correspondence.

What if I need to change my supervisor?

Submit a query explaining the reason, current status of work, and any deadlines. We will advise the next steps based on policy.

Is this also for short projects (not a full thesis)?

Yes—if it falls under departmental UG projects/thesis guidance, use this tab.
