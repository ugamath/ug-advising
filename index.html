Frequently asked questions

Quick answers
Which tab should I use?

Choose the tab that best matches your query. If you are unsure, use Other UG Matters. On the form, you can also describe the issue and we will reroute internally if needed.

Can I email directly instead of using the form?

Yes. You can email uga.math@snu.edu.in. The form is recommended because it collects key details in a consistent format.

Is this site official?

This is a departmental advising portal created to streamline communication. Official university policies still apply; when needed, we will direct you to the Dean's Office / registrar process.
