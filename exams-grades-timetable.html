FAQ

Common questions
I missed an exam. What details should I send?

Provide course code, instructor, date/time of the exam, reason for absence, and any supporting document (medical note, travel proof, etc.).

I think my grade is incorrect. What’s the process?

First check with the course instructor. If unresolved, submit your query with the course code, component (midterm/final/etc.), and a clear description of the concern.

Who should I contact for timetable clashes?

Use this tab and include both course codes and the nature of the clash (overlap, back-to-back room issue, etc.).
